Work from Home Mothers

Work from Home Mothers community is a cohort of women who work from home (including stay at home mums). It comprises family women with diverse expertise who desire to expand their network by building meaningful relationships, boost their self-esteem, improve their work-life balance and build profitable businesses to global standards

The Governor - Ibukun Omololu

Ibukun Omololu is an author, a public speaker, podcaster, and a seasoned banker with over 14 years of professional experience. She has garnered expertise across critical areas such as Customer Service, Relationship Management, Strategic Sales & Marketing, Treasury Management, and Compliance. At Sterling Bank Plc., Ibukun headed the One Woman proposition, providing strategic oversight on the Women Banking Desk of the bank. She functions in adequate capacity, designing solutions tailored to the needs of the women customer segment, enabling access to financial services. A major highlight of Ibukun’s passion for socio-economic development was championing the launch of The Bloom Network – an initiative designed to groom and optimize leadership capabilities for women across essential business areas at Sterling Bank. This among other achievements culminated in winning the prestigious 2017 CBN Sustainability Award for Women Banking. She is driven by a goal to successfully transform access to financial services, investment opportunities, and capacity building for women, their families, and businesses. Ibukun holds a B.A in Linguistics from the University of Ilorin and is a Certified Retail Banker from the Prestigious Retail Banking Academy, U.K. She is happily married to Adedayo Omololu and blessed with two beautiful boys. Ibukun loves children, enjoys traveling and exotic food experiences. She has a pet peeve for dogs and a sweet tooth for cake.
