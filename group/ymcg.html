Young Men’s Community Group

The Young Men’s Community is an initiative of TCN aimed at raising and mentoring young men to rise up to their position and responsibility as men in society in accordance with God’s word. The community focuses its activities on Faith, Leadership, Family, Purpose, Productivity and Evangelism
